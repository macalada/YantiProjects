Studio's Policies

Parents & students are responsible for ensuring they arrive on time and are picked up on time. Please ensure that the instructor is notified at least 24 hours before class if your child(ren) cannot attend. This year, we have implemented a 3-strike policy. If students are more than 10 minutes ate to class or miss a class without informing the instructor, they will receive a strike. Lates/absences after the third strike will be subject to a $5.00 fee.
Students may bring NUT-FREE snacks to class and, are required to bring a water bottle. Snack restrictions may be put in place if other students have allergies. Please make sure to clean up after yourself. If students do not maintain respectful eating habits, snack permissions may be revoked.
Fees for classes are charged monthly. Fees must be paid in full by the end of the second class of every month. A reminder will be sent out before each due date. Payments can be made by cash or etransfer. Overdue payments will be subject to a 5% surcharge weekly. Outstanding fees may result in request of withdrawal and removal of the student from the year-end show. There will be no refunds for missed or cancelled classes. If personal financial difficulties arise during the year, please contact Inaya directly to make payment arrangements. To ensure accessibility, Abhinaya Dance Studio has partnered with Kidsport. KidSport provides grants to children from families facing financial barriers so they can participate in registered sport programs.
All students are required to attend our photo day. More information will be provided closer to the date. Every student will require a costume(s) for performances including the year-end performance. The studio is responsible for designing all costumes and we will do our very best to keep the costumes as affordable as possible. The studio will purchase all fabrics, accessories, etc. needed for all performances. The studio however, will not be responsible for any costume items lost or stolen after costumes have been sent home. Students are responsible for supplying their own tights, undergarments, hair accessories and make-up -as needed costumes will be not be given out unless full payment is received. No exceptions. Students will not be allowed to perform without paying for costumes in advance.
Abhinaya Dance Studio will be involved in many performances and will attend and host many events in the upcoming year, including team builders. Each month there will be a team building activity that will help the Abhinaya Family create a stronger sense of camaraderie. Team building activities are not included in monthly class fees and are subject to additional fees. Below are some of the events/performances that will take place this year: Stampede breakfast and Parade, Tie-Dye Party, Movie and Pizza Party, Abhinaya Dance Retreat, Abhinaya Dance Beach Party, Abhinaya takes on Edmonton. The year-end show gives all the students the opportunity to perform on stage in front of a large audience - in front of friends, family, and others. Each class performs in the show; performance pieces will be rehearsed during regular class time, however, extra rehearsals may be organized closer to the show date. Dancing on stage is a unique experience that helps to build confidence and allow the parents to see their children dance in a professional venue. More details regarding the year-end show will be shared with parents closer to the show date.
Abhinaya Dance Studio prides itself in being an inclusive and safe environment for students, parents, and staff. There is a strict NO TOLERANCE policy for bullying, gossiping, and shaming. This extends to parents as well. Parents are allowed to voice their concerns, however, we request parents to be cooperative and trust the talent and decision-making ability of studio staff. We will try to handle all conflicts within the studio. If this fails, a call will be made to the parent and a meeting will be arranged. We expect that all students will be on their best behaviour in class and will be respectful of other dancers within the school, the teachers, the premises, and the others. When student are performing outside the studio we expect them to be on their best behaviour, to be courteous to competitors, other teachers, and members of the general public. Students and/or parents who repeatedly disrupt classes, who conduct themselves in a manner that the studio ownership deems to be objectionable, or who cause reputational damage to the studio will be expelled from the studio.
